This Year's Topic

The topic for this year's convocation is " The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania"
as the reflection on the topic of this year's African Statistics Day on November 18.

The theme is "Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA): The role of official statistics
and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Africa"

Awards events

During convocation ceremony at Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre, outstanding students and leaders of the EASTCSO are recognized through special gift presentation. This ceremony honors their exceptional academic achievements and exemplary leadership roles within the student community.
